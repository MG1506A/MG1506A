Biography

Mel Grasse is an artist and designer from Mauritius. He was born and raised in the city of Curepipe in 2000. He specializes in imaginative character work. Most of his work on shown on his Instagram page under the name of @Keons_Creation and Website.

He is inspired by cartoons and anime, street art and graffiti, childlike imagination, comics, world cultures and folklore.
Currently a university student, he spend his free time to create and expand his knowledge on his craft.
Mel Grasse is creating his own weird and mysterious world one piece at a time.
